- **Name**: Soybeans for sprouting (Kongnamulkong)
- **Other Names**: 콩나물콩
- **Type**: Legume / Sprouting bean

## Description

These are soybeans specifically selected for sprouting into kongnamul (soybean sprouts). They are larger and more robust than regular soybeans, ensuring strong, healthy sprouts.

## How to use

- Used to grow soybean sprouts (kongnamul)
- Can be cooked and added to rice blends

## Substitutions

If unavailable, use regular soybeans, though sprouting success may vary.

## Storage

Store dried beans in a cool, dry place. Use promptly for best sprouting results.
